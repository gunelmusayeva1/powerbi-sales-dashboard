<h2>📌 About This Project</h2>

<p>In this project, I built an interactive <b>5-page Sales Dashboard</b> using Power BI.
The dashboard analyzes sales performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments.</p>

<h2>📈 Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
  <li><b>Best region:</b> Central — 308K total sales, 51.50% profit margin</li>
  <li><b>Lowest sales:</b> South region — however, its profit margin is higher than some regions with more sales</li>
  <li><b>Recommendation:</b> Increasing sales volume in the South region should be a priority, as its high profit margin means revenue growth would be significant</li>
  <li><b>Product categories:</b> Clothing, Electronics, Furniture, Groceries</li>
  <li><b>Customer segments:</b> Retail, Wholesale, Online</li>
</ul>
